  <strong>What We Do</strong>

  - Information support – access to practical information on moving to, living, and working in Armenia, including personal experiences and advice from repatriates and expats through our website and social media platforms;

  - Online and in-person consultations – guidance on integration, employment, business opportunities, networking, education, healthcare, and legal matters;

  - Employment facilitation – access to a dedicated repatriate sub-platform on staff.am and personalized employment guidance;

  - Community and networking events – formal and informal gatherings, introductions to impactful organizations and initiatives, and group excursions across Armenia;

  - Health insurance package – preferential health insurance options designed for members of our community;

  - Free Armenian language courses – structured language classes for repatriates at different proficiency levels;

  - ՀայTalki – Armenian Speaking Club – a community-based space for practicing spoken Armenian and building confidence in everyday communication.
