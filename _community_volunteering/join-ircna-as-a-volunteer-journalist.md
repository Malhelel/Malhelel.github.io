  Volunteer with IRCNA

  Join a community‑driven, independent newsroom and help shape the future of English‑language journalism in Armenia. As an IRCNA volunteer, you won't just be an observer—you'll be part of a team that believes the people living the story should help tell it.

  What you can do as a volunteer:

  · Report on local stories from your own town or region
  · Submit tips, photographs, and event information
  · Help research and verify facts for ongoing investigations
  · Translate content to reach wider audiences
  · Support community outreach and networking events
  · Participate in IRCNA‑led training sessions and workshops

  Who we're looking for:
  Anyone with a passion for truth, a respect for community, and a willingness to listen before they write. No prior journalism experience is required—curiosity and commitment matter more than credentials.

  Volunteering with IRCNA is unpaid and remote. It's a chance to give back, build real skills, and be part of a platform that is funded by its community—not by political parties or corporate owners.
