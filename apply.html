Join IRCNA

What to expect when you apply

Thank you for your interest in volunteering or joining as an independent journalist. Before you fill out the form, please take a moment to read the following important policies:

  • No Politics Policy – IRCNA is strictly non‑partisan. Volunteers may not use the platform for political campaigning or advocacy.
  • Volunteer Journalist Terms of Service – This document outlines your rights, responsibilities, code of conduct, financial transparency, and intellectual property licensing.
  • Content, Privacy & Inquiry Policies – Learn how we handle content usage, your personal data, and external inquiries.

By submitting your application, you confirm that you have read, understood, and agree to these policies. Our team will review your application and get back to you via email.
