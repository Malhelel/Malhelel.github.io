IRCNA Volunteer Journalist Terms of Service

Effective Date: June 22, 2026

By applying to volunteer as a journalist for IRCNA (Independent Reporting and Community Networking in Armenia), you ("Volunteer") enter into this agreement. These terms are designed to protect IRCNA’s reputation as an independent, non-partisan, and community-driven organization.

1. The IRCNA Standard: Values and Conduct

Volunteers represent the IRCNA brand. By accepting this role, you agree to:

  • Unbiased Journalism: Adhere to the core IRCNA value of reporting facts without partisan spin. Your reporting must be verified, objective, and told without fear or favor.
  • Community-First Approach: Recognize that IRCNA’s journalism is rooted in listening. Engage with the community respectfully and ensure voices are treated with dignity.
  • Non-Partisanship & No Politics Policy: IRCNA is an independent, non-partisan initiative. Volunteers must avoid taking partisan stances or engaging in political campaigning while representing IRCNA.

2. Journalistic Expectations

  • Pitch and Approval: All field reporting must be pre-approved via a brief pitch to IRCNA administration.
  • Editorial Review: IRCNA reserves the right to edit, decline, or remove any content that does not meet our journalistic standards.
  • Independence: You act as an independent contributor and are solely responsible for your physical safety and conduct.

3. Financial Policy and Fundraising

  • No IRCNA Commission: IRCNA never takes, requests, or receives any cut, fee, or commission from a Volunteer’s fundraising.
  • Strict No-Solicitation: IRCNA will never solicit or take a percentage of donations made to its Volunteers.
  • Personal Fundraising Transparency: Volunteers must include this disclaimer: "Donations go directly to the Volunteer to support their independent reporting and do not constitute a donation to IRCNA as a corporate entity."
  • Independence from Third Parties: IRCNA is not sponsored or partnered with any third-party payment services (e.g., Idram). Use of such services is personal; IRCNA holds no affiliation with these providers.

4. Intellectual Property and Licensing

By submitting work to IRCNA, the Volunteer grants IRCNA a non-exclusive, perpetual, royalty-free license to host, distribute, edit, and promote the work on all IRCNA platforms.

5. Legal and Arbitration

No Employment Relationship: This is an unpaid, voluntary position. No employment, agency, or partnership is created by this agreement.

Arbitration Clause: Any dispute arising out of these Terms shall be settled by binding arbitration in Yerevan, Armenia, in accordance with the rules of the Arbitration and Conciliation Center at the Chamber of Commerce and Industry of the Republic of Armenia. The arbitration shall be conducted in English.
